CASTING MATERIALS

Castable Resin Properties

Our castable resin burns out cleanly at 750–850°C with zero ash residue — critical for defect-free precious metal castings.

  • ● Jewelry-grade castable resin — zero-ash at 800°C
  • ● High-detail dental castable for crown frameworks
  • ● Industrial castable for high-melt alloys (platinum, cobalt)
  • ● Wax-blend resin for traditional casting house compatibility
  • ● Large-format patterns up to 250 × 200 × 200mm
  • ● Spruing, tree-building, and burnout support available
